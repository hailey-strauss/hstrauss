About Me

Hailey Strauss is a photographer, videographer and social media specialist who will graduate in May, 2023 from High Point University with a Bachelor’s Degree in Social Media and Digital Communications. Hailey’s technical skills include infographics as well as video and graphic design. Utilizing Adobe Illustrator, Adobe In-Design, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects and Canva has become second nature to her. Hailey is also proficient in Excel, PowerPoint, video lighting and sound, plus she has the ability to navigate multiple social media platforms.

Hailey is a High Point University photographer who showcases events which are included on the university’s website, social media accounts and marketing materials. She has also served as a marketing intern for Goodroot, a pharmaceutical formulary company, working with the marketing team to design, produce and edit videos of the company’s leadership team members’ critical thought processes which were displayed on the company’s social media accounts. She has carefully produced videos and created graphics while interning for several non-profit organizations and she worked as an art specialist with young children. Hailey also directed, edited and acted in a full-length film

Hailey’s leadership and volunteer community activities include: High Point Universtiy Leadership Fellows, High Point University softball team player and team photographer, University Choir, and WishMakers. Hailey has completed over 300 hours of volunteer work for non-profit organizations which provide services to children, families and elders in community.
